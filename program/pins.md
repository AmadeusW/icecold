# Pins


## Input

* 2 - Joystick L
* 2 - Joystick R
* 1 - Sensor Scored
* 1 - Sensor Lost
* 6 - Limiters

## Output

* 7 - Motor driver
* 2 - shift register
* ? - RGB strand

### Shift register
* 10 - Lives indicator
* 10 - Level indicator
* 10 - Target LEDs

### Parallel to Serial for input
* 1 - Sensor scored
* 1 - Sensor Lost
* 6 - Limiters
